Pineapples

A pineapple is a relatively pointy yellow fruit with a sour inside

Did you know?

  1. Pineapples are technically a collection of berries fused together
  2. They contain an enzyme called "bromelain" that breaks down proteins in your mouth. So, when you eat pineapple, pinapple eats you back
  3. There are two main types of pineapple, the "cayenne pineapple" and the "red Spanish Pineapple"
