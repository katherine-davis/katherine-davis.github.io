PB&J Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 tbsp Strawberry Jelly
  • 2 tbsp Peanutbutter

Instructions:

  1. Lay the two slices of bread out
  2. Put jelly on the face of one slice of bread
  3. Put peasnutbutter on the face of the other slice of bread
  4. Stick bread pieces together, covered faces pointin towards each other
  5. Use knife to cut diagonal line from one corner to the corner opposite
  6. Enjoy!
