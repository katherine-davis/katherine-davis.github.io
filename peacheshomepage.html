Peaches

A peach is a round orange fruit of the south. They have pits instead of seeds

Did you know?

  1. Peaches are also known as the "Fruit of Calmness", because they have elements in them that can reduce anxiety
  2. There are two types of peaches, "Clingstones" and "Freestones"
  3. Each year, a peach tree can produce up to 66 lbs of fruit
