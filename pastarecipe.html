Pasta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Jar of Tomato Sauce
  • Noodles
  • Water
  • Basil Leaves
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Put salted water in a pot and bring to boil
  2. Put noodles in boiled pot and cook until done
  3. Heat your tomato sauce
  4. Drain noodles
  5. Combine noodles with sauce
  6. Sprinkle basil as wanted
  7. Enjoy
