Pasta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of Bisquick
  • 1 cup of Milk
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 tsp Vanilla
  • 1 tbsp Sugar
  • A squeeze of Lime Juice
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 cup Chocolate Chips
  • 1/2 cup of Syrup
  • 1 tbsp Butter

Instructions:

  1. Mix wet and dry ingredients together separately
  2. Combine and add chocolate chips
  3. Heat 1 tbsp butter in a pan
  4. Pour batter into discs in pan
  5. Cook until bubbly and flip
  6. Put on a plate, add syrup, and enjoy!
